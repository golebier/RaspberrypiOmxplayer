
Key bindings
Key	Action
1	Increase Speed
2	Decrease Speed
j	Previous Audio stream
k	Next Audio stream
i	Previous Chapter
o	Next Chapter
n	Previous Subtitle stream
m	Next Subtitle stream
s	Toggle subtitles
q	Exit OMXPlayer
Space or p	Pause/Resume
-	Decrease Volume
+	Increase Volume
Left	Seek -30
Right	Seek +30
Down	Seek -600
Up	Seek +600
